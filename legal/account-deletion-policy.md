# Account and Data Deletion Policy – GymOpsHQ / GymSaaS

Effective date: 28 April 2026

This page explains how users can request account deletion and data deletion for GymOpsHQ / GymSaaS.

## 1) How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your account and related personal data, send an email to:

- admin@gymsaas.com

Use subject: **"Account Deletion Request – GymOpsHQ"**

Please include:

- Your full name
- Registered phone number
- Registered email address (if any)
- Gym name
- Clear statement that you want account deletion

## 2) Verification

For security, we may ask for additional verification to confirm you are the account owner or an authorized gym admin before deletion.

## 3) What We Delete

Upon successful verification and request processing, we delete or anonymize:

- Profile/account data
- Attendance records linked to your account (where legally permissible)
- Billing and invoice records linked to your account (where legally permissible)
- Uploaded photos/documents
- Push notification device tokens

## 4) What May Be Retained

Some records may be retained for legal, accounting, audit, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution requirements.
Retained data is limited to what is required by law or legitimate business necessity.

## 5) Processing Timeline

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within **30 days**.
If more time is required due to legal/technical reasons, we will notify you.

## 6) In-App Deletion

If in-app self-service deletion is added in future releases, this policy will be updated with exact steps.

## 7) Contact

For deletion requests or status follow-up:

- admin@gymsaas.com
