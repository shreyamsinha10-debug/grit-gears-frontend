# Privacy Policy – GymOpsHQ / GymSaaS

Effective date: 28 April 2026

GymOpsHQ ("we", "our", "us") provides a gym management mobile application and related services ("App").
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use the App.

## 1) Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

- Account and profile data: name, phone number, email address, membership details, batch, attendance status.
- Billing and payment data: payment status, dues, invoice history, and related transaction metadata.
- Device and technical data: app version, device type, operating system, crash/diagnostic logs (if enabled).
- Optional uploaded data: profile photo, ID document images, and other documents you voluntarily provide.
- Push notification data: device push token (for delivering gym messages and reminders).

## 2) How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

- Create and manage gym member/admin accounts.
- Provide core features (attendance, billing, invoices, plans, and member dashboard).
- Send service notifications such as reminders, announcements, and payment updates.
- Improve app performance, reliability, and security.
- Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

## 3) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.
We may share data only with:

- Service providers needed to run the app (for example cloud hosting, database, and push notification providers).
- Authorized gym administrators for operations within their own gym account.
- Law enforcement or authorities when required by law.

## 4) Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for service operation, legal compliance, accounting, dispute handling, and security purposes.
When no longer required, data is deleted or anonymized.

## 5) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information.
However, no internet transmission or storage method is 100% secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 6) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access your personal data.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Withdraw consent where applicable.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

## 7) Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for gym management and is not directed to children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8) International Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers located in different countries.
By using the App, you consent to such processing and transfers where legally permitted.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
